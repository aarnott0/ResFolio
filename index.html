Introduction

Lets start by being aware of the difference between a portfolio and a resume.

A portfolio is a collection of knowledge and material that offers a visible example of a person’s skilled field.

It contains in-depth data on a person’s skills, expertise, and achievements. Since it’s one of these, it’s long and
takes longer to make.

A portfolio is employed to impress the employers in an interview and receive the job by showcasing that the candidate
is an expert with all the relevant skills.

A resume is a document that summarizes a person’s abilities, achievements, and skills.

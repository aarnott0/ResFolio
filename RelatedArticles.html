Tips

The art industry is competitive and so it may take multiple exposures to your work to get a lead. Don't get discouraged!

Don't just create art in class. Draw, paint, and create in your everyday life! These are the pieces that reviewers are really
interested in seeing because they highlight your interests, passions, and creativity outside of an instructor's requirements
or classroom setting.

When comparing your work with others, remember that your goal is not for your work to look exactly like theirs, but it is
to constantly improve your talent and skills as an artist.
